Introduction

Middle Earth : Shadow of Mordor serves as a prologue to Lord of the Rings tilogy. This action/adventure open world game was developed by Monolith studios and distributed by Warner Bros.The story revolves around Talion , a ranger seeks revenge on murder of his family. This has garanred crirtical acclaim. IGN gave 9.3/10.

Review

I got my hands on this 3rd person open world game on last month. It looks pretty much like Assasain's creed to me. Few of my friends also got mistaken this as an Assassin's Creed game. But this is not a rip-off of Assassin's creed.I found out that on the 1st hour of gameplay. This added novelty to this genere. The beautifully crafted fighting mechanism is mind blowing, so is the dynamically changing opposition , in this case Sauron's army.This mechanism is called "Nemesis". If you get killed by a higher rank of Sauron's army many events will take place within the army.Same will apply if you kill a high rank in the army.This game offeres nearly 20 hours of gameplay which is really cool.

I was tired of playing poorly optimized games.Can't blame developers though.Recent adoptation of 1 year development cycles for popular titles made them(devs) code really really fast. But this game seems pretty optimized. No crashing, no lagging ,nothing. I played this game on my hp- pvilion 15 core i3 with Radeon graphics. To my ammusement i could play this in medium settings with a decent frame rate. So thumbs up monolith you have nailed it :)

My thoughts on this game is pretty high. If i were asked to give marks i would give 10 out of 10 for this
